The ATI Radeon is a video card that comes bundled with later Power Mac G3s, several Power Mac G4s, and the eMac.

This covers three generations of the cards:

## R100
* Max video memory: 64 MB
* Most cards in this series include hardware-based Transform & Lighting
* Advanced Z-buffer

## R200
* Max video memory: 128 MB

## R300
* Max video memory: 256 MB
* Programmable shaders
